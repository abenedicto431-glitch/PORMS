Before you apply

What to prepare

Have these ready before filling out the form to make the process faster.

🏢

Business Registration

DTI, SEC, or CDA registration number and year. Also prepare your company registration date.

👤

Owner Information

Owner or authorized representative's full name, birthdate, sex, contact details, and valid email address.

📊

Financial Data

Initial capital, annual production volume, estimated production value, and employment count by category.

⚡

Energy Records (EMP)

Past 12 months of electricity bills (kWh & PHP) and fuel consumption logs if applying for EMP.

📋

Farm Data (APP)

Commodity list, varieties/breeds, farm area per crop, average yield, income per cropping season.

📧

Valid Email Address

You will receive a confirmation email with your reference number and a PDF copy of your submitted form.
